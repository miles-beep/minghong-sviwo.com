Conception of our cooperation model

In the founding period of “Minghong”, the main element was to use the “red” of the Chinese representative.
We hope that more people will understand the meaning of “red” and then our symbol “made for honor” is born.

Our company was established in 1995, and consists of three factories,
namely Sichuan Minghong Vehicle Manufacturing Co., Ltd., Sichuan Lihong New Energy Co., Ltd.,
and Sichuan Hanlin Automotive Manufacturing Co., Ltd., which are responsible for the production,
assembly and development of various types of products.

We have the most advanced production lines in southwest of China,
high temperature dust-free coating lines, off-site welding robots, etc.
We believe that we can provide more people with green clean energy
transportation and transportation tools, provide more employment opportunities,
and make more users willing to own an electric car.




Joining cooperation process

1、Environmental protection: The electric vehicle adopts power battery pack and a motor-driven power, does not generate exhaust gas or emit exhaust gas during work. It is almost "zero pollution" which is beneficial to environmental protection and air cleanliness.

2、Low noise: The electric vehicle does not make noise like traditional vehicle, and the noise generated is almost negligible.

3、Affordable: The cost of electric vehicles is only about one-fifth of gasoline vehicles. Moreover, the energy conversion efficiency is high, and at the same time, the braking and downhill energy can be recovered to improve the energy utilization efficiency.

4、Easy to maintain: The electric vehicle uses electric motors and batteries to drive. Comparing the cumbersome maintenance projects for traditional engine, such as: change the oil, filter, belt and so on, electric vehicle only needs to periodically check few components like the motor battery.

5、Battery Recycling: people can reconfigure the batteries, and apply them to some places with low magnification, such as remote controls, electric bicycles, etc.
