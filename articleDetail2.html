Minghong in Thai-Sichuan Cooperation Conference

On May 23, 2018，Thai-Sichuan Cooperation Conference on Science and Technology Innovation co-sponsored by the Sichuan Provincial People's Government and the Ministry of Science and Technology of Thailand was held in Bangkok, Thailand. Peng Yuxing, deputy governor of the Sichuan Provincial People's Government, and Gan Yawen, deputy minister of science and technology of Thailand, attended and delivered speeches. The two parties held a signing ceremony for the cooperation project, and Minghong Vehicle successfully signed a contract with the Thai company DT Motor.

In order to promote the Sichuan “One Belt and One Road” science and technology action plan and promote the “going out” of advanced technology and equipment, Minghong Vehicle and Thai company DT Motor successfully “hand in hand” to jointly develop the Thai electric vehicle market. Sichuan Minghong Vehicle Co., Ltd., as a manufacturer specializing in the production of electric tricycles and electric four-wheelers in Sichuan, has the largest spraying line and electrophoresis line in Southwest China, and the annual output of electric tricycles reaches 200,000. At present, the Thai market is mainly gasoline tricycles and motorcycles, while electric tricycles are less, and the market has great development potential. Through the “matchmaking bridge” of the previous city science bureau, Minghong Vehicle Company and the Thai company DT Motor hit it off and signed a cooperation agreement at the Thai-Sichuan Cooperation Conference on Science and Technology Innovation. According to the agreement, the two parties will take advantage of their respective advantages in the market and technology, jointly form a joint venture company, assemble and sell electric vehicles in Thailand, and strive to achieve win-win cooperation.
