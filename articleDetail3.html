Minghong welcomes the visit of Cuban officials

Minghong hosted officials from the Cuban government on August 2, 2018. Accompanied by Minghong staff, they visited Minghong's factory and workshop production line.

At the same time, the officials visited our latest product ---Aries power and took a ride. Aries Power is the latest three-wheeled six-seater electric car launched by Minghong vehicles in August, made with the latest technology, the new power system, super horsepower, can carry six people at a time. 

At the meeting, the officials expressed their high enthusiasm for Aries power. Cuba’s Minister of Industry pointed out that Minghong’s green vehicles are exactly what the world needs. At the end of the trip, Minghong staff and government officials took a group photo.
