Our team in Suining Factory

Our team in Suining has some of the nation’s most famous electric tricycle since 1995. Today, more than 800 Chinese engineers, designers, craftsmen, and production workers build and operate sophisticated, custom equipment that produces millions of preeminent e-cars and tricycle every year.

Creating a new standard

The electrical car factory went into production at the plant in Sichuan in 1995. Between then and now, some 1 million T-series vans have been built here. Every day, a total of around 120 vehicles roll off the production line. We have the most advanced pressing facility, the bodywork shop, and the assembly line.

By owning one of the world’s best electric tricycle factories, we’re able to break the decades-long cycle of big brands overdesigning and overcharging and do things the Harry’s way–focused on high quality, fair prices and customer satisfaction.


Worldwide offices

At Minghong, we work seamlessly together as one firm to serve our clients wherever they need us. We have 17 offices in 8 countries around the world. We make our collective knowledge, experience and global network available to our clients.
